about me

My name is Ahmed. I love food, fitness and techology.

I was born and raised in Egypt until 2007, when I relocated with my family to the United States.

DBC is a four-phase bootcamp spread out over 19 weeks. It was a very diverse and educationalatmosphere which helped me grow technically and personally. It was a life time experience.

I learned plenty of different programming languages and frameworks. I learned about myself, how to work with teams, and most importantly that I can learn anything if I set my mind to it.
